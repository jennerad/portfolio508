Creative Space

I am a curious person and have always been interested in the inner workings of people. When I meet a creative person, I immediately begin asking questions: How did you get started? What inspires you? What is your process? I want to place myself in their shoes for just a moment. This curiosity led me to begin a series of work where I make environmental photographic portraits of creative people working in their creative spaces.

I have had the good fortune to meet and photograph numerous dedicated artists and craftspeople who find time and space to feed their creative spirit.
